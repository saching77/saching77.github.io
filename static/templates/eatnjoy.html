Eat n' Joy
Multi-cuisine Dining Chain

Overview

As a restaurant owner, it becomes equally important to focus on customer interactions as they are more valuable to you. “eat n joy” is one of the leading multi-cuisine dining chains in town; offers new menu options, largely based on multinational cuisine and uses seasonal customer preferences to introduce new dishes. Core values prioritize being customer-focused to provide excellent service and employee-focused by maintaining a superior quality work environment. When a customer shares a pic having dinner at your restaurant, it generates much more value for your brand than when he likes one of your posts on social media. Research shows that a fully engaged customer represents more percentage of revenue than average. An engaged customer is more likely to buy from your brand, offer feedback, and develop loyalty toward the brand.

Domain:
Hospitality
Business Model:
B2C
Type:
Mobile Application
Scenario:
Reserve a table

UX Impacts

  • Increased Customer Response
  • Lower Bounce Rate
  • Increased Revenue

Some of the Digital and Non-Digital Customer Engagement Ideas allow to Build a Loyal Customer Base

Persona

Scenario

John decides to spend an evening with his family after his office hours. He calls Ana, his wife about the plan and asks her too to search good restaurant where kids and everyone can enjoy and spend a good time with good food. John and Ana find the best-suited restaurant in town “eat n joy”. Eat n joy offers attractive discounts + Reward points on Dine treats, which also add value in the rememberable experience of the pleasant atmosphere of the restaurant. Since they had been to “eat n Joy restaurant earlier, without making any other choices John decide to go again “eat n joy”. Knowing the regular rush in the evening at EnJ, he reserves the table from his mobile application. With provided facilities in the App.
He can:

  • Reserve the table
  • Select the Date and Time according to the guest's convenient
  • Choose the Seating
  • Select the menus
  • Food dishes can be viewed in a real-time environment (optional),
    he can also select the menu in real time.
  • Redeem the rewards, Corporate offers
  • Receive the communication via SMS, e-mail
  • He should get updates on his reservation time by time
