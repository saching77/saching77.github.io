OVERVIEW

Sajag Financial Services-Sajag Securities is a well-diversified firm offering a range of financial products and services founded in 1995, a member of the National Stock Exchange and the Bombay Stock Exchange. It is the depository participant of NSDL and CDSL & a Mutual Fund Distributor. Market leader in retail broking with 31k + individual client base

EVALUATION

Branding
The current brand logo is mixed with multiple items/objects. Red and Gray colors are representing high contrast in their pair. Hence getting visibility disturbance.

Theme
The current color theme is “Red” which has a range of symbolic meanings including war, anger, and danger. When people become angry their faces become flushed with color.

Accessibility
Overall navigation is lengthy and included unnecessary flows. Haven't thought about POUR principals.

Trend
Overall application experience gives an outdated feel.
